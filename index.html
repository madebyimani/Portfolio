✨ About Me

Hi, I’m Imani — a self-taught, aspiring front-end developer currently building my very first web projects from the ground up.

I recently made the decision to shift into tech with a strong focus on web development. What started as curiosity quickly turned into a passion for creating clean, user-focused websites. As a self-taught developer I am committed to learning every day — at this time I’m focused on HTML and CSS, and I’m excited to dive into JavaScript and React as I grow.

Right now, you’re looking at my very first live page — my “Portfolio” I’ll be adding a Projects section soon where you’ll be able to explore what I’m working on, and how I built them.

While my current focus is front-end development, my long-term goal is to become a full-stack developer so I can build complete, end-to-end web applications. I’m especially interested in roles that allow me to grow as a developer while contributing to real-world solutions.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy being with my family, watching movies, and trying new foods and resturants.

Thanks for stopping by — and check back soon to see what I build next!
